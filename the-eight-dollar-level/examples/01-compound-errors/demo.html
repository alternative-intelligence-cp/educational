üî® The Construction Analogy

Imagine you're framing a wall. You check your level and the bubble is "in the marks" - not dead center, but close enough. Maybe 1/16" off.

Wall 1: 1/16" off - barely noticeable
Wall 2: 1/8" off - starting to compound
Wall 3: 3/16" off - getting worse
Wall 4: 1/4" off - door doesn't fit

You just spent 8 hours building walls you now have to tear down. 16 hours of work because "close enough" compounded.
